Tiramisu':

This delicious tiramisu made with homemade mascarpone custard (no raw eggs), layers of whipped cream, and rum and coffee-soaked ladyfingers is an impressive Italian dessert. Dust the top of the tiramisu with cocoa powder before serving.

    Ingredients:
  • mascarpone
  • savoiardi
  • cacao
    How To:
  1. Make the Filling: Cook the egg yolks, sugar, and milk until slightly thickened.

  2. Make the Whipped Cream: Beat heavy cream with vanilla extract until stiff peaks form

  3. Soak the Ladyfingers: Combine coffee and rum in a small bowl.

  4. Assemble the Tiramisu: Line the bottom of a baking dish with soaked ladyfingers
